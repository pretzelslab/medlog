# MedLog — Personal Health Journal

A privacy-first, single-file personal health tracking app. No backend, no server, no data sent anywhere — everything lives in your browser.

## Features

- **Dashboard** — Overview of your health activity with stats and recent events
- **Log Events** — Record doctor visits, medications, surgeries, checkups, symptoms, and more
- **History** — Browse, search, and filter all past health events
- **Symptom Tracker** — Dedicated tracking for symptoms with severity levels
- **Analysis** — Visual charts and insights of your health trends over time
- **Multi-Profile** — Manage health records for multiple family members under one account
- **Share** — Share health summaries via Gmail, WhatsApp, and other channels
- **Authentication** — Secure login with OTP verification

## Tech Stack

- Pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies to install
- [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) for health trend visualizations
- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) — Playfair Display & DM Sans
- Data stored locally in browser `localStorage`

## Getting Started

1. Download `medical-life-log.html`
2. Open it in any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
3. Sign up and start logging your health events

No installation, no internet connection required after the first load.

## Privacy

All your health data is stored locally in your browser's `localStorage`. Nothing is sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will erase your records — export regularly to back up.
