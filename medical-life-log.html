About MedLog
MedLog — Personal Health Journal
Version 1.5 · Built 2026 · Browser App

MedLog is a private, browser-based health journal for individuals and families. All data stays on your device.

Key Features: Multi-member family tracking, medical events, symptom logging, yearly analysis, timeline grouping, enhanced search, share via Gmail/WhatsApp/SMS/PDF, backup and restore.

Technology: Single HTML file, no installation. Built with vanilla JS and Chart.js. Designed with Claude by Anthropic.

Disclaimer: Not a medical device. Always consult a healthcare provider for medical decisions.
